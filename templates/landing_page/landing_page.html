The TaDa Web App

A productivity web app for keeping track of your schedule and what you need to do! "It's not to-do, it's TaDa. Magical to get anything done."

Sign-In

To sign-in, click the Sign-In button and authenticate with Google. Upon login, you will be able to create notes and calendar events. When you re-enter the application after log-out, your saved notes and events will automatically populate.

Note

When you create a note by pressing the 'Add Note' button, you can add a title and text and move it around. Delete a note by clicking the 'X' button. To save, click the 'S' button. After saving, click the 'E' button to save any future edits you make to your notes. Click the 'C' button to select a background color for your notes.

Calendar

Clicking the 'Add New Event' button will open up a modal that will help you choose a title, start and end time, and a color (optional) for your new event. After saving, the event will appear in the calendar which you can then click to make any future edits or to delete.

Weekly View

Weekly view is provided along with monthly view. Buttons to switch between view are in the top right corner.

Note-Calendar Integration

Saving a date in the description of your notes will make an event appear on your Calendar. This event will have the same title as the corresponding note and the same color as your note as well. Clicking on these events will not open the modal; any edits you want to make to this event must happen in the note itself. Examples of text which translates to a date are but are not limited to: "Tomorrow at 3 PM to 4 PM", "This Friday at 10 AM", "11/20/2017 at noon", etc.

Bug Report


To report a bug, click the link on the right for the GitHub issues page.
1) Click the "New Issue" button and insert the title according to the format: "Bug Report: (content)".
2) In the description, tell us about the bug that you found.
3) On the right, label the issue by clicking the label and bug.
4) To finish the report, click the "Submit New Issue" button.
